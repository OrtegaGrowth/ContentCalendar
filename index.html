✦ FAQ

Common questions

What platforms do you create calendars for? +
We build calendars for Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and Pinterest. Starter covers one platform; Growth covers up to three; Pro covers all of them.
How long does it take to receive my calendar? +
Most calendars are delivered within 24–48 hours after you complete your intake brief. During busy periods it may be up to 72 hours, but you'll always be notified upfront.
Will the content ideas actually sound like me? +
Yes — that's the whole point. Your intake brief covers your voice, tone, niche, and audience. Every idea is tailored to you. And if something doesn't feel right, revisions are always included.
Do I need a large following to work with you? +
Not at all. OrtegaGrowth was built for small businesses and influencers at every stage. Whether you have 500 or 50,000 followers, a solid strategy is what drives real growth.
Can I come back every month for a new calendar? +
Absolutely — and many clients do! There's no subscription required. Just re-order when you're ready for your next month, and we'll already know your niche and voice.
